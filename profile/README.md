# Protocol Institute

The Protocol Institute is a context tank — an independent research organization devoted to furthering the study and application of protocols, and furthering protocolization of the world.

We evolved from the [Summer of Protocols](https://web.archive.org/web/20260421142108/https://summerofprotocols.com/), a research program funded by the Ethereum Foundation (2023–2024), which brought together over eighty researchers across multiple disciplines to investigate the deep structure of protocols as a category. The Institute carries that work forward through ongoing research, publishing, convenings, and mentorship.

**Website:** [protocol-institute.org](https://protocol-institute.org) &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Magazine:** [protocolized.io](https://protocolized.io)

## Repositories

### [website](https://github.com/Protocol-Institute/website)
The Protocol Institute organization website at [protocol-institute.org](https://protocol-institute.org). Plain HTML/CSS/JS, no build step.

### [protocolized-website](https://github.com/Protocol-Institute/protocolized-website)
The website for [Protocolized](https://protocolized.io) — the Protocol Institute's magazine and research library. Built with Astro and Tailwind. Hosts 285+ research resources (papers, essays, talks, games, datasets, fiction) from the Summer of Protocols archive and beyond, with daily sync from the Protocolized Substack.

### [c3po](https://github.com/vgururao/c3po) *(migrating to this org)*
C3PO is the Protocol Institute's RAG research assistant — a conversational agent trained on the full research corpus. Named for the *Star Wars* protocol droid. Built on Voyage AI embeddings, Pinecone, and Claude. Will be integrated into the Protocolized research library once deployed. Currently in development at [vgururao/c3po](https://github.com/vgururao/c3po); will be transferred here on release.
